Welcome

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Cincinnati working on the development of metal-based photosensitizers for cancer therapy. My research focuses on photodynamic therapy (PDT), excited-state redox processes, and biomedical applications of transition metal complexes.

I earned my Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of Alabama in 2025, where I developed multifunctional phototherapeutic systems targeting cancer cells.
